# ðŸ“– Project Description

### What is our idea? GreenStep is a daily eco-habit tracking prototype that gamifies climate action. It allows users to easily log their daily environmentally friendly actions (such as taking public transit, bringing a reusable tumbler, or turning off unused lights) to earn points and visualize their personal carbon footprint reduction.

### Who does it help? This application is specifically designed for Taylor's University students and staff. Many students want to contribute to environmental sustainability but struggle to track their impact or stay motivated. GreenStep helps the campus community stay engaged in climate action through a simple, rewarding feedback loop.

# Our team followed an Agile/Scrum methodology over a 4-week period to develop this prototype.
